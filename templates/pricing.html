FAQ

What is a "lead pack"?

A lead pack is a pre-configured industry filter — e.g., IT & Software, Construction, Consulting. Each pack contains relevant CPV codes used to query the TED EU tender database.

Can I switch lead packs?

Pro plan includes 1 active pack at a time. You can switch any time. Agency plan gives you access to all packs simultaneously.

Do I need a Stripe account?

No — Stripe is used for payment processing on our end. You just provide your credit card details during checkout.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Register for free to explore the dashboard and see sample tenders before committing to a paid plan.
